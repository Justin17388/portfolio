About Me

Hi, I'm Justin — a hands-on problem solver with an Applied Engineering degree in CADD and OSHA safety certifications. Outside of work, I am a dedicated father of three, fully immersed in their activities — from band competitions with the Oxford High School “Sound of Champions,” to helping with cheer practices, concession stands, and a full lineup of dance recitals covering ballet, baton, twirl, and tap. Family means everything to me, and being involved is a top priority.

When I’m not cheering from the sidelines or behind the concession grill, I enjoy exploring new technologies. I spend my free time learning code, diving into generative AI, and playing bass guitar and synthesizers. I also run a small 3D printing business focused on creating sensory fidgets for individuals with ADHD, Autism, and other neurodivergent conditions — blending creativity with purpose.

Professionally, I manage the website and marketing for my wife’s counseling practice, where I apply both creative and technical skills. Before that, I spent 12 years at a sports manufacturing company, where I led engineering process improvements, maintained OSHA compliance, and built powerful Excel tools to analyze data pulled from enterprise databases. I’m currently working toward AWS/DevOps certification through Clarusway and actively building my skills with additional courses on Udemy. My goal is to become a full-stack developer with strong cloud and automation capabilities.

Thank you for visiting my site — I’d love the opportunity to connect or collaborate!

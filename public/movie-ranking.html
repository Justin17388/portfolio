The Best Movies According to Justin

My top 3 movies of all-time.

JAWS

This is my favorite movie of all time. The way the atmosphere and score are used to overcome the special effects capabilities of the time is absolutley masterful.

Saving Private Ryan

My grandfather was a WWII veteran and he used to tell me stories of what he went through. This movie does an amazing job capturing that storytelling spirit.

Die Hard

Because you know it is not officially Christmas until Hans Gruber falls off Nakatomi Plaza Tower. I'm just saying.
